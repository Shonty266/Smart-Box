About Us

At Smart Goods using IOT, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the way goods are delivered, combining innovative technology with user-friendly design. Our flagship product, the IoT Box, is a sophisticated web-based application that creates a secure and seamless delivery system for online shoppers and sellers alike. Our mission is to empower users with the ability to effortlessly reserve a designated box, securely place their items, and enjoy a reliable delivery process that keeps them informed every step of the way. By integrating cutting-edge features such as item detection sensors, unique QR code generation, and real-time GPS tracking, we ensure that our customers experience unparalleled convenience and peace of mind.

Our commitment to transparency and security is reflected in the entire delivery cycle, from the moment an item is placed in the IoT Box to its safe arrival at the recipient's doorstep. With integrated communication modules, both senders and recipients receive timely notifications, ensuring they stay connected throughout the process.

Upon arrival, the recipient simply presents the generated QR code to unlock the box, facilitating easy access to their package. Whether returning the box to the sender, directing it to a nearby collection point, or handing it back to delivery personnel, we have designed our solution to be intuitive and efficient for all stakeholders. Join us as we redefine delivery logistics, making it secure, dependable, and efficient for everyone involved.
